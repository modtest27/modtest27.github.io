    About US

    HISTORY

    Founded in 1995, Power-Fact has dedicated its existence to providing companies with solutions for their mission-critical environments.

    We have grown continuously since our inception, and we attribute this success to providing excellent service and support. We work closely with you to ensure all your needs are met.

     

    Building Relationships Based on Superior Service

    CULTURE

    The core foundation at Power-Fact is teamwork. We believe that working as a team with our customers is what leads to both our success and our customers’ success over the years.

    We pride ourselves in having a positive attitude; taking ownership of issues, problems, and opportunities; treating others with respect, and doing everything it takes to meet our commitments.

    Every employee at Power-Fact is uniquely trained to take pride in their work and provide our clients with a support level that exceeds expectations.
