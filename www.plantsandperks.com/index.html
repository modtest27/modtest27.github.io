    How it works

    Incentivse

    We reward your employees for engaging and learning through our platform

    We underpin our technology with perks and gamification with clear incentives and motivations for hitting sustainability goals.

     

    Employers see tangible behaviour change as employees enjoy charting their positive progress and reaping the high value rewards.

    Inform

    We empower employees with the knowledge to implement healthy and sustainable habits

    By tracking employees current behaviour we can serve relevant educational content, quizzes and bite-sized facts wherever they are.

     

    This empowers employees with the knowledge to implement healthy and sustainable choices when it matters most.

    Inspire

    We energise your employees with fresh content and innovative, sustainable products

    We introduce new concepts and habit breakers to employees looking for fresh ideas: from innovative products to sample to fresh planet-friendly recipes.

     

    This motivates employees to make changes to their lives for personal and planetary health and to share their inspiration with those around them.
