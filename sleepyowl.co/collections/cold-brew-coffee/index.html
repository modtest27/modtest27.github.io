The Story of our Cold Brew Coffee

Cold Brew Coffee is at the heart of Sleepy Owl's story. We had started off our journey brewing this niche overnight coffee, batches after batches from our founders' apartment back in 2016. While it was sold as On-Tap Cold Brew Coffee Boxes initially, we developed a way to help people brew the same black coffee at home!

Several years later, we are proud to say that we established ourselves on the back of this absolutely delicious, smooth, less bitter, and almost 70% less acidic Cold Brew. While the wait is longer for this one, the overnight brewing only adds to the whole experience of the Cold Brew Coffee as we know it. Each Brew Pack makes up to 3 large cups, giving you the ultimate pleasure of the perfect iced coffee.

Our Cold Brew Coffee comes in 8 flavours- Original, Dark Roast, Cinnamon, Hazelnut, Mocha, Caramel, French Vanilla, and New Orleans. You can have it black or with milk, and you can even heat it up if you do not wish to have it cold.
