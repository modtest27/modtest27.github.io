Customers Often Ask—

  • How long do I need to brew the coffee for?

    You need to brew your coffee for a minimum of 5 minutes. If you like your coffee stronger, you can brew it for longer.

  • How many cups of coffee can be brewed from one bag?

    Each Brew Bag makes one solid cup of coffee!

  • What is the caffeine content in your Hot Brew Bags?

    Our Hot Brew Bags have approximately 105 mg caffeine per bag.

  • Can we reuse the bags once already used?

    Our Brew Bags are designed to make one solid cup of coffee. We don’t recommend using these again.

  • What is the shelf life of your Hot Brew Coffee?

    Our Hot Brew Coffee has a shelf life of 12 months from the date of manufacturing.

  • What flavour notes does the French Vanilla Hot Brew have?

    Our French Vanilla Hot Brew has its own sweetness that comes from the rich vanilla flavour. When blended with the chocolatey notes of the 100% Arabica beans, it offers a taste like no other making the coffee smell as good as it tastes. 

  • Is the vanilla flavour in the French Vanilla Hot Brew natural?

    Yes absolutely! We don’t use any preservatives or additives in our coffee, so whatever you taste is completely natural and pure, straight from the source.
