Customers Often Ask

  • What is Xpresso Coffee?

    Xpresso Coffee is a premium, strong coffee by Sleepy Owl that's designed to provide you with a rich and intense espresso-like experience. It's not for the weak hearted but perfect for those who love a strong and bold coffee.

  • Which beans do you use for Xpresso?

    Xpresso is made of 100% Premium Robusta Beans.

  • How is it different from regular coffee and coffee brands?

    Xpresso is specifically crafted to be stronger and more intense than regular coffee. It's made using a unique blend of high-quality coffee beans, resulting in a bolder flavour and higher caffeine content.
